# EA Intent and Workflow

This document outlines the end-to-end workflow involved in the EA's operation, including the processes that lead to trade entries, invalidations, and scoring mechanisms.

## Workflow Steps

1. **H1 Divergence**  
   Identifies high-level divergence patterns on the H1 chart.

2. **Strict CHoCH**  
   Confirms a strict change of character in momentum.

3. **M5 Retest/Reclaim**  
   Waits for a retest or reclaim of significant areas on the M5 chart.

4. **M5 Micro-BOS**  
   Looks for a micro break of structure on the M5 for entry confirmation.

5. **Entry/Invalidation/Score**  
   Determines entry points, invalidation levels, and scoring based on predefined criteria.

6. **KPI**  
   Tracks key performance indicators as metrics for evaluation.
